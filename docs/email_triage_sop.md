# Email Triage SOP (Phase 0 — Manual)

No automation in Phase 0. Prove the habit first.
14 days of consistent execution is the email gate.

## Daily Procedure (Target: 20 minutes max)

### Inbox 1: fabian.diaz@trafficaccess.com.au (TAS Business)
- Process every unread email
- Flag action items
- Flag commitments made or received

### Inbox 2: fabian@fdnxt.com (Personal + Forwarded TAS)
- Same process
- Flag anything that belongs in a PIL register (Phase 1)

### Rules
- Anything requiring a response today — respond or delegate before closing triage
- Anything taking more than 2 minutes to process goes into the action list — not the inbox
- No email stays unread after triage: archive, action, or delete

## Phase 0 Success Criteria
- Both inboxes triaged daily for 14 consecutive days
- Time target: 20 minutes maximum for full triage
- This is a discipline gate, not an automation gate
